Food order app

ROLE:UX researcher, UX designer, UI designer    
Platforms: Android mobile application       
Tools:User research, Figma, Sketch, Invision, Zepplin
OverView:
A resturant wanted a mobile food ordering application of a single design, and with user-friendly interface.
Food ordering apps are quite popular these days.
I decided to create competing app where to make an order should be as simple as few clicks on mobile device and it should be easy to understand and informative about the options and choices the users have.

Gathering info (Research & interviews)
The research phase started with goggling competitor applications in Greece, in UK and USA. Analyzing and comparing the content of their apps helped me to determine the direction of development for my application.
Further, to build empathy with users, I started off with a set of casual interviews.
This resulted in a preliminary set of requirements and creating User Personas.
Interviews helped me to discover list of main requests of the users:
  • Quality of the service
  • Good choice of listed restaurants
  • Delivery/Take away option
  • Price criterion
    • Also buy reading User Reviews of competitor apps i was able to spot and evaluate problems that they were facing, so my design will be ahead for the competition. Then I did analysis of colours, their perception and influence. The Green is a cool color that symbolizes nature and the natural world. Perhaps because of its strong associations with nature, green is often thought to represent tranquillity, Good Taste Researchers have also found that green can improve reading ability.
